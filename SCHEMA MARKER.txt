<li class="opt">      <span face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><span face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif"><input class="rad" name="question6" type="radio" value="A" />&nbsp;</span>3 Ω</span></li>

<div class="answer">
<span face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Solution</span><br />
<span face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Resistance of original wire is
R=ρ l/A
ρ,being the specific resistance of wire. When the wire is cut in two equal halves then resistance becomes
R<sup>'</sup>=(ρl/2)/A=R/2
Thus, the net resistance of parallel combination of two halves is given by
R<sub>net</sub>=(R<sup>'</sup>×R<sup>'</sup>)/(R<sup>'</sup>+R<sup>'</sup> )
          = R<sup>'</sup>/2=R/(2×2)=6/4=1.5Ω
</span><br />
